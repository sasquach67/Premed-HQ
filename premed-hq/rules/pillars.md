# Pillars — purpose, subtabs, must-haves

Sidebar groups: Home · Foundation · Experiences · Your Story · Application (reversible). Each pillar = its own page with subtabs and side-peek panels. Tips embedded per pillar (delivered by the mascot, not boxes). Resources = categorized clickable cards (new tab).

## Home / Overview
Greeting tied to current-term roadmap (no source labels). Top bar surfaces quote-of-day + MCAT Q-of-day. **Live "next event" countdown widget** (NCSSM-Time style, from Google Calendar) over the Ghibli hero. Task bar (tasks only, with checkboxes; finishing archives). Today's Focus (editable). Category segmented bar + rings. AMCAS roadmap card w/ countdowns. Recent activity. Archive view for finished tasks/focus (Reminders-style, restorable) on its own route.

## Academics & GPA
Subtabs (keep this lineup): **Planner · GPA · Tar Heel Tracker · Resources & Notes**, reordered by priority.
- **Main dashboard first = Assignments Tracker + synced Calendar view** (Notion model) + link to notes.
- GPA: AMCAS engine — per-course BCPM/AO toggle → cumulative + science + AO (4.0, no grade replacement, repeats averaged) + what-if simulator + grade-weight what-if + rings.
- Tar Heel Tracker = degree planner (grad + major reqs), seeded from real UNC data.
- Transfer credit: collapse behind a button → side-peek/own tab (not a long inline list).
- Notes = mini database of pages opening in a side-peek (not a scratchpad).
- Tables: ensure text fits.

## MCAT (needs the most work)
- **Tab bar at top:** Schedule · Score Tracker · Error Log · Resources. Remove the 4 useless top squares.
- Schedule = phased **visual timeline/guide** (Content Review → Practice → Full-lengths → final), not checkboxes.
- Error Log = structured filterable **cards** per missed question (topic, section, why-missed, fix, source) + quick-add. Not a table-with-textarea.
- Resources = Content Review / Practice / Full-length groups; each item a clickable link to a tab/page (placeholders OK).
- Score Tracker = minimal (low priority).

## Experiences (Clinical · Volunteering · Shadowing · Research)
Hour logs as detailed tables (date, site, role, contact, hours, notes) + rings. Research embeds the Google Drive folder (not a URL box).

## Extracurriculars (rebuild — NOT an hours tracker)
A **per-organization database**: one entry per club/sport/org → profile with type, role, experiences/reflections, opportunities, calendar/events, links. Placeholders fine.

## Your Story (Essays & Story Bank)
Keep the loved pattern: a card/tab → click → opens a real writing space (side-peek/editor). Story bank w/ tags + personal-commentary + seeded reflection prompts. PS workspace (analysis checklist, drafts, editing resources). Secondary-prompt tracker. Apply this open-to-write pattern wherever a textbox exists today.

## Application (Letters · School List · Timeline · Profile)
- Letters: recommender, relationship, status, deadline, thank-you.
- **School List = its own database**, disconnected from Academics; fix wasted space; text fits.
- Timeline = visual graphic (not a list).
- Profile: organized sectioned layout, editable fields sync to store, editable avatar (import/photo/drag-drop). Drop the long "relevant coursework" list.

## Help
A Help tab housing the Discord + support/"everything" links.
