Strong vs Weak Acids & Bases

0/14 · 0:00

0 pts

Sort each species into the correct category. Remember the seven strong acids and the strong (alkali/alkaline-earth hydroxide) bases; everything else ionizes only partially.

Strong acid

Ionizes ~100% in water

Weak acid

Partially ionizes; Ka << 1

Strong base

Dissociates fully to release OH-

Weak base

Partially accepts H+; Kb << 1
