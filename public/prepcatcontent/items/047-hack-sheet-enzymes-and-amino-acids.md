## Enzymes and amino acids

Kinetics, inhibition, and the amino acid facts that show up on test day.

### Enzyme kinetics
v = Vmax[S] / (Km + [S])← Michaelis Menten
→
Km is the [S] at half of Vmax. A low Km means high affinity.
→
Competitive inhibitor: Km up, Vmax same. Beat it with more substrate.
→
Noncompetitive inhibitor: Vmax down, Km same. Extra substrate cannot rescue it.
★
Competitive Competes at the active site.

### Amino acids
pI = ½(pKa₁ + pKa₂)← the two pKa around the neutral form
v = Ez / f← electrophoresis migration velocity
→
At pH below pI the amino acid is net positive; above pI it is net negative.
★
Acidic: Asp and Glu (D, E). Basic: Lys, Arg, His (K, R, H).

### Energetics
ΔG = −RT ln K_eq
→
Enzymes lower activation energy and speed both directions. They never change ΔG or K_eq.
