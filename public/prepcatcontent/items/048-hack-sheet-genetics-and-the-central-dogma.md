## Genetics and the central dogma

From DNA to protein, plus the population math that trips people up.

### Central dogma
DNA → RNA → protein← transcription, then translation
→
Replication is semiconservative. Leading strand continuous, lagging strand in Okazaki fragments.
★
Purines are A and G (Pure As Gold). Pyrimidines are C, T, and U.

### Hardy Weinberg
p + q = 1      p² + 2pq + q² = 1
→
2pq is the carrier frequency, q² is the homozygous recessive frequency.
→
Given the recessive phenotype, take q = √(q²), then p = 1 − q.
!
Assumes no selection, no migration, no mutation, random mating, and a large population.

### Inheritance
→
Two carriers (Aa × Aa) give 1/4 affected and 1/2 carriers.
→
X linked recessive traits show up far more in males, who carry a single X.
★
Mitosis makes 2 identical cells. Meiosis makes 4 unique gametes.
