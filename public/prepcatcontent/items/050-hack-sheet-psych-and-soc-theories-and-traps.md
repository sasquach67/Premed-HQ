## Psych and soc, theories and traps

The theorists, the schedules, and the biases that all sound alike.

### Theorists and development
★
Piaget stages: Sensorimotor, Preoperational, Concrete, Formal (Some People Can Fly).
→
Erikson is psychosocial and spans the whole life. Freud is psychosexual and ends in adolescence.
→
Classical conditioning (Pavlov) is involuntary. Operant conditioning (Skinner) is voluntary.

### Reinforcement
→
Reinforcement increases a behavior, punishment decreases it. Positive adds, negative removes.
★
Variable ratio schedules resist extinction the most. Think slot machines.

### Memory and bias
★
Memory goes encoding, then storage, then retrieval.
→
Fundamental attribution error: blame others for their character but excuse yourself with the situation.
!
The self serving bias protects your ego. Do not mix it up with the actor observer bias.
