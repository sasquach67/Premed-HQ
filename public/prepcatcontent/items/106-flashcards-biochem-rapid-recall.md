QUESTION

What is the rate-limiting enzyme of cholesterol synthesis, and what regulates/targets it?

Tap to flip

ANSWER

HMG-CoA reductase. It converts HMG-CoA to mevalonate in the cytosol/ER and is the target of statins. It is activated by insulin and inhibited by glucagon (via phosphorylation) and by cholesterol/sterols (SREBP regulation).
