## Waves, sound, and light

Sound, optics, and everything that oscillates, boiled down to the formulas.

### Waves and sound
f = 1/T      v = fλ
I = P/A← intensity in W/m²
β = 10 log(I/I₀)← sound level in dB · I₀ = 10⁻¹²
β_f = β_i + 10 log(I_f/I_i)
f' = f (v ± v_d)/(v ∓ v_s)← Doppler · closer raises pitch
open pipe: λ = 2L/n      f = nv/2L
closed pipe: λ = 4L/n      f = nv/4L
→
Closed pipes carry only the odd harmonics (n = 1, 3, 5).

### Light and optics
c = fλ      E = hf← light is an EM wave
θ₁ = θ₂← law of reflection
n = c/v← index of refraction
n₁ sinθ₁ = n₂ sinθ₂← Snell law
θ_c = sin⁻¹(n₂/n₁)← critical angle
1/f = 1/o + 1/i = 2/r← thin lens and mirror
m = −i/o      P = 1/f← magnification, lens power (diopters)
lenses in series: 1/f = Σ 1/fᵢ      m = m₁ × m₂
→
Converging lens or mirror has f > 0. A positive image distance means a real, inverted image.
