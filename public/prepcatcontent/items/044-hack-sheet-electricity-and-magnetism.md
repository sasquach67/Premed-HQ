## Electricity and magnetism

Charges, circuits, and fields, the formulas you cannot blank on.

### Charges and fields
F_e = k q₁q₂ / r²← Coulomb law
E = F/q = kQ / r²← electric field
U = kQq/r      V = kQ/r← potential energy, potential
E = V/d← uniform field between plates
ΔV = W/q← voltage · work per charge

### Circuits
I = Q/t      V = IR
P = IV = I²R = V²/R
series: R = ΣR      parallel: 1/R = Σ 1/R
ρ = RA/L← resistivity
C = Q/V = ε₀ A/d← capacitance
U = ½ C(ΔV)²← energy stored in a capacitor
series C: 1/C = Σ 1/C      parallel C: C = ΣC← opposite of resistors
→
Two resistors in parallel are always less than the smaller one.

### Magnetism
F_B = qvB sinθ← magnetic force
F = qE + qvB sinθ← Lorentz force
