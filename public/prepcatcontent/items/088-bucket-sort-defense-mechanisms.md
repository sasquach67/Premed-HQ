Defense Mechanisms

0/14 · 0:00

0 pts

Ego defense mechanisms reduce anxiety by distorting reality, but they vary in psychological maturity. Sort each defense into Mature (adaptive, healthy), Neurotic (intermediate, common but less adaptive), or Immature (primitive, more maladaptive).

Mature

Adaptive, healthy coping that channels impulses constructively

Neurotic

Intermediate defenses common in everyday life; reduce anxiety but distort reality

Immature

Primitive defenses linked to poorer adjustment and greater reality distortion
