## CARS strategy and traps

No formulas here. Just how to read, what each question wants, and the traps.

### Reading the passage
→
Read for the argument and the tone, not the details. Details you can always go back and find.
→
Watch the shift words: however, but, yet, although. The main point usually sits right after them.

### Question types
→
Main idea: the answer restates the thesis, not one supporting example.
→
Inference: the answer must follow from the passage, using no outside knowledge.
→
Strengthen or weaken: pick the choice that adds or removes support for the conclusion.

### Answer traps
!
Extreme words like always, never, and only are usually wrong in CARS.
!
Beware answers that are true in the real world but never supported by the passage.
→
Stuck between two? Pick the one that stays closest to the passage scope and tone.
