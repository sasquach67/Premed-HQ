## Kinetics, electrochem, and structure

Rates, cells, and the atomic and bonding odds and ends.

### Kinetics
rate = k[A]ˣ[B]ʸ← orders come from experiment
k = A e^(−Ea/RT)← Arrhenius · higher T, faster k
!
Reaction order is not the stoichiometric coefficient. You read it off rate data.

### Electrochemistry
E_cell = E_red − E_ox
E_cell = E° − (RT/nF) ln Q← Nernst
mol M = It / nF← electrodeposition
★
OIL RIG: Oxidation Is Loss, Reduction Is Gain of electrons. Anode oxidizes, cathode reduces.

### Atoms and bonding
max electrons in a shell = 2n²
KE = hf − φ← photoelectron · φ is the work function
FC = V − NB − ½B← formal charge
p = q · d← dipole moment
[α] = α_obs / (c × l)← specific rotation
